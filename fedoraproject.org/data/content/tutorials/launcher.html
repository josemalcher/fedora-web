Tutorial: Launcher

In this tutorial we will create a custom desktop Launcher for Firefox. So the first step we need to do is right click on your desktop. Click on the "Create Launcher". After that we will keep the type as "Application", Name will be "Firefox", Command is in this example will be "/usr/bin/firefox" or you can click on the browse button and locate the executeable for Firefox by going to /usr/bin/firefox. In the comment field you can type anything its not mandatory. For my example i will input "Browse the internet". Click ok and you will find a new launcher on your Desktop. Hope you enjoyed this fun little tip.
